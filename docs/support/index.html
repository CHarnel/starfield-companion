Starfield Companion by Alex Margulis

About the App

Starfield Companion is a mobile reference app for the video game Starfield. Browse and search a database of skills, items, weapons, planets, crafting recipes, and more — all on your phone. The app works offline with bundled game data and requires no account or sign-in.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. All game data is bundled with the app for offline use. An internet connection is only needed to load some images on first view (they are cached for later) and to check for app updates.

Is the app affiliated with Bethesda or Microsoft?

No. Starfield Companion is an unofficial fan project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Bethesda Game Studios, ZeniMax Media, or Microsoft.

Does the app collect my personal data?

No. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, analytics, or tracking. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Report a Bug or Request a Feature

If you've found a bug or have a feature request, the best way to let us know is by opening an issue on GitHub:

github.com/CHarnel/starfield-companion/issues

Please include a description of the issue, your device model, and OS version so we can investigate effectively.
