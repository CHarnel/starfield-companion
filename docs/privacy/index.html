Privacy Policy

Starfield Companion by Alex Margulis

Effective March 18, 2026

Overview

Starfield Companion is an offline reference app for the video game Starfield. It lets you browse and search bundled game data on your phone. The app does not require an internet connection to function, does not have user accounts, and does not collect personal information.

Data Collection

Starfield Companion does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

There are no accounts, no sign-ins, no forms, and no user-generated content. The app does not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, location, or any other on-device data. No unique device identifiers are tracked.

Network Access & OTA Updates

The app connects to Expo's servers solely to check for and download over-the-air (OTA) application updates. These requests include only non-identifying technical metadata:

  • Device operating system (e.g. "iOS 18" or "Android 15")
  • A randomized installation token (not a device identifier)
  • The developer's project ID (identifies the app, not the user)
  • The current update ID (so the server knows whether a new update is available)

No unique device identifiers or personal information is transmitted in these requests. No other network requests are made by the app.

Third-Party Services

Expo EAS Update is the sole third-party service used by the app, exclusively for delivering OTA updates. Expo acts as a data processor, is GDPR- and CCPA-compliant, and receives no personal data from app users. For details, see Expo's privacy documentation.

Analytics & Advertising

The app contains no analytics, no crash-reporting SDKs, no advertising networks, and no tracking pixels. We do not track how you use the app.

Children's Privacy

Because the app does not collect any data from any user, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). No special measures are needed because no data is collected from anyone.

Data Security

All game data is bundled within the app at build time and stored locally on your device. OTA update checks use HTTPS encryption in transit. Since no personal data is collected, there is no personal data at risk.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices change in the future, this policy will be updated and the effective date at the top will be revised. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
